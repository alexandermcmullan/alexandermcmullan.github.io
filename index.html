About Us

Alexander and McMullan is a firm based in Ballymoney that provides a wide range of accountancy services including tax returns, annual accounts, payroll and bookkeeping.

We believe that our strength is putting our client's interests first. Our objective is to provide a high-quality cost-effective service appropriate to their needs.

We work hard to get to know our clients so that we can deliver timely individual advice helping you improve your business and personal wealth. We have many clients in the local community and service businesses, small and large, across many sectors.

Whatever your needs, we are here to help.

Services

At Your Service

Accountancy

We can prepare accounts for all types of businesses, from sole traders to partnerships and limited companies. We pride ourselves on being able to produce your accounts promptly to ensure everything is in the right format, in the right place and at the right time.

Bookkeeping

Bookkeeping is a dependable way of managing all your accounts and help to make them flourish. By having an experienced bookkeeping accountant, you are gaining a wide range of advantages to manage and control your finances, plan for future growth and ensure that your accounts and records meet all legal requirements.

Payroll

Running a payroll can be time-consuming and complicated. We would be happy to discuss the implications of employing someone and the responsibilities faced by employers from payslips, returns, certificates to dealing with HMRC in real-time requirements, we can guide you through the processes, costs, calculations and deadlines.

Pensions

As well as payroll services we also comply with the legal requirements for pension legislation. Automatic enrolment places duties on employers to automatically enrol 'workers' into a work based pension scheme. We can provide advice on choosing the most suitable pension schemes and registration with the pension regulator.

Tax

People and businesses are subject to a wide range of taxes including Income, Corporation, Capital Gain, Inheritance, Stamp Duty, Value Added Tax and National Insurance. Where tax is concerned we recognise the importance of considering the implications of all taxes on your circumstances. Our job is not simply to calculate the taxation due and tell you what to pay but also make you aware of any tax saving solutions you could benefit from.

Self assessment

We have the expertise to take the headache out of the form filling and compliance aspect of Self Assessment. HMRC have increased the penalties for failing to file a return on time and for errors. We can complete tax returns, calculate any tax liability and advise you on exactly when to make payments and how much to pay.

VAT

The changing regulations and the growing demands of VAT compliance can be a difficult administrative process. Whether you're just starting out or have been in business for a long time. We can help with Registration, De-Registration, VAT Returns and corresponding with HMRC on your behalf.

Corporation Tax

The legal responsibility for correctly calculating the corporation tax liability falls on business owners. We understand the issues facing owner-managed businesses, therefore, we will calculate your company's tax liability, prepare tax returns, help to minimise corporate tax exposure and relieve the administrative burden of this compliance.
